Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find the answers you're looking for? Contact Us.

Do I really need a hearing test if I feel like I hear okay?

Many hearing changes happen gradually. A baseline hearing test helps identify early changes and protect long-term hearing and brain health.

Are hearing aids only for older adults?

No. Hearing loss affects people of all ages, and early treatment leads to better outcomes.

How are you different from big-box hearing aid stores?

We provide medical-level care, personalized treatment, and long-term support, not just device sales.

Will hearing aids help my brain health?

Yes. Research shows that treating hearing loss supports cognitive health and reduces listening fatigue.
