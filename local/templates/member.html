Practice Owner & Director of Audiology

Dusty Jessen, Au.D.

Dusty Jessen is an audiologist providing hearing tests, hearing aids, and other hearing care services in Littleton, CO.

Clinical Leadership and Practice Background

Dr. Jessen has served as the director of audiology at Columbine Hearing Care since 2002. She purchased the practice in 2017 and, in 2025, expanded her work as the owner of New Leaf Hearing Care in Arvada, CO. 

She is committed to best-practice care and strong outcomes, and she keeps her practice intentionally small so each patient receives the time, attention, and personalized support they deserve.

Education

Dr. Jessen earned her Master of Science in Audiology from Arizona State University and her Doctorate in Audiology from the Arizona School of Health Sciences.

Credentials and Professional Involvement

She is certified by the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association (ASHA) and holds an active Colorado Audiologist and Hearing Aid Provider license. 

Dr. Jessen is a Past President of the Colorado Academy of Audiology and is a member of the Academy of Doctors of Audiology, the Academy of Rehabilitative Audiologists, the Entheos Audiology Cooperative, and the Hearing Loss Association of America. 

She also serves on the board for the Denver Chapter of the Hearing Loss Association of America.

Community Giving and Access to Care

Dr. Jessen is passionate about making hearing healthcare more accessible. She is a founding member of Hearing the Call – Colorado, a non-profit that provides hearing aids and hearing healthcare for underserved individuals in the local community.

Patient Education and Aural Rehabilitation

Her focus on education and counseling led her to write and publish a patient handbook, Frustrated by Hearing Loss? 5 Keys to Communication Success.

The book is used by hearing care providers worldwide and is a key part of her aural rehabilitation program, 5 Keys to Communication Success.

Personal

A proud Colorado native and devoted dog lover, Dr. Jessen and her family have fostered many homeless dogs over the years. She lives in Centennial with her husband, Ben, and their two sons, Caleb and Brody.

Communication Training Program

At New Leaf Hearing Care, we take a holistic approach to hearing health through aural rehabilitation, combining hearing technology with education, coaching, and practical communication strategies to help you stay connected and confident.

LACE AI Pro

We offer LACE AI Pro as part of our holistic hearing rehabilitation approach. This app-based auditory training program helps strengthen your listening skills, especially in noisy environments, group conversations, and fast speech, so you can get real-world benefits from your hearing technology over time.

Tinnitus

At New Leaf Hearing Care, we provide personalized tinnitus counseling and management to help reduce the impact of ringing or buzzing in your ears. After a thorough evaluation, we'll identify likely contributing factors and create a practical plan that may include education, sound therapy options, hearing technology support when appropriate, and strategies to improve comfort, sleep, and day-to-day focus.

Custom Ear Molds

Custom earmolds are made specifically for your ears to improve comfort, fit, and performance. At New Leaf Hearing Care, we create custom molds for hearing aids, hearing protection, and select assistive devices, helping reduce feedback, improve sound quality, and ensure a secure, comfortable fit for everyday use.

Ear Wax Removal

What Makes New Leaf Hearing Clinic Different

Locally owned, independent audiology practice

You’re not a number here. Our care is built around relationships, trust, and long-term support.

Audiologists with personal experience with hearing loss

Comprehensive care

Hearing healthcare should improve your daily life, not just your hearing test results. We look at the full picture: listening environments, communication needs, cognitive wellness, and comfort.

Education, counseling, and long-term support
