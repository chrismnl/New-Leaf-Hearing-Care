ReSound Hearing Aids

  • ReSound hearing aids are modern hearing devices designed to help you hear speech clearly and stay comfortable in busy, real-life listening situations.
  • ReSound offers options like Bluetooth LE Audio and Auracast streaming, hands-free calling on supported devices, and app tools for fine-tuning and tinnitus relief.
  • New Leaf Hearing Care provides ReSound hearing aid fittings and support in Arvada, CO and Littleton, CO.

ReSound has a long track record of building innovative hearing solutions designed to fit naturally into everyday life. Since real listening happens in busy places - at home with family, at work, and out in the community - ReSound focuses on technology that supports clearer speech and easier communication in the moments that matter most.

With a strong emphasis on smart features and forward-thinking design, ReSound works to help people stay connected, confident, and fully engaged in life.

These are the hearing aid models available from ReSound.

ReSound Enzo IA

+

The ReSound Enzo IA is designed for severe to profound hearing loss. It is the world’s smallest rechargeable Super Power hearing aid and the first of its kind to support Bluetooth LE Audio and Auracast broadcast streaming.

With a stylish, durable design, the ReSound Enzo IA is built for the most challenging listening situations. It offers exceptional clarity, feedback control, and all-day battery life.

Unique Perks and Features:

  • Clear Focus: Automatically reduces background noise while highlighting speech.
  • 360 All-Around listening: Naturally shifts attention between voices and surrounding sounds.
  • Comfortable and weatherproof: Lightweight, all-day wear with protective nanocoating.
  • Flexible connectivity: Supports Bluetooth® LE Audio, Auracast™, and direct streaming from multiple devices.

ReSound Vivia

+

ReSound Vivia™ is a smart hearing aid that helps you hear better and enjoy the sounds around you. It uses Intelligence Augmented (IA), which works with your brain to make hearing easier. Whether you are in a noisy place or a quiet room, ReSound Vivia helps you hear clearly and stay focused on what you want to hear.

ReSound Vivia™ also features advanced noise reduction and speech enhancement, allowing you to focus on conversations even in challenging listening environments. Its adaptive technology continuously adjusts to your surroundings, ensuring a natural and immersive hearing experience. Designed for all-day comfort, ReSound Vivia™ provides reliable performance, so you can stay connected and engaged in every moment without distraction.

Unique Perks and Features:

  • Bluetooth Connectivity: ReSound Nexia connects to the new Bluetooth LE Audio, providing the purest sound quality and minimal battery usage for continuous streaming.

ReSound Savi

+

ReSound Savi™ is a smart hearing aid that helps you hear clearly and stay connected every day. It combines the best sound technology with small, comfortable designs and easy Bluetooth® connections. ReSound Savi is made to give you natural sound, all-day comfort, and a simple way to hear better in any situation.

ReSound Savi™ automatically adapts to different environments, reducing background noise and enhancing speech clarity so you can focus on the sounds that matter most. Whether you’re in a bustling café, a quiet park, or on an important phone call, its advanced technology ensures a smooth and effortless listening experience. ReSound Omnia ITE

+

ReSound’s Omnia family of hearing aids also offers custom in-the-ear (ITE) devices that are designed to fit your unique ear anatomy. This allows you to enjoy all the benefits of modern hearing aid technology in a smaller package. One of the benefits of choosing a custom device is that it is naturally shielded from wind noise, as they sit inside the ear canal. Additionally, ITE devices do not interfere with glasses or other items worn behind the ears.

ReSound Omnia in-the-ear (ITE) products are currently available in a variety of sizes to meet your unique fitting preferences. The types of custom ITE products include: in-the-canal (ITC), completely-in-the-canal (CIC), and invisible-in-the-canal (IIC). These devices are appropriate for fitting mild to severe-to-profound hearing losses. They offer either rechargeable or disposable battery options.

Frequently Asked Questions About ReSound Hearing Aids

What are the levels of ReSound technology?

+

The newest ReSound Omnia hearing aids offer 3 technology levels (9, 7, and 5), ranging from the highest to the lowest technology level, respectively.

How do I adjust my ReSound hearing aids?

+

ReSound hearing aids can be adjusted by pressing the buttons on the hearing aids, or through the ReSound Smart App. Pressing the buttons will allow you to change volume and programs, while the Smart App allows you to make these changes as well as adjustments to sound quality, noise reduction, and much more. Your audiologist or hearing care provider will teach you the best way to adjust your hearing aids for your unique needs.

What is the life expectancy of ReSound hearing aids?

+

The typical lifespan of a completely new hearing aid is 5 years. Largely, it depends on how well you take care of your hearing aids. Some patients will decide to upgrade their hearing aids prior to this time, as newer technology is routinely released and may provide greater benefits. An audiologist will be able to guide you in deciding when an upgrade is necessary.
